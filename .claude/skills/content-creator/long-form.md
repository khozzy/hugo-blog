# Long form content guidelines

**Purpose**: Establish authority, SEO, email capture
**Length**: 1,500-3,000+ words
**Frequency**: 1-2 per month (quality over quantity)
**Characteristics**:

- Timeless ("Lindy Effect" - will be relevant in 5 years)
- First-principles reasoning
- Concrete examples from real experience
- Clear takeaways for the reader
- Diagrams/visuals where helpful

For deep articles, propose:

- Title options (specific > clever)
- Hook/intro approach
- Key sections outline
- Core argument or insight
- CTA (email signup, comment, share)
